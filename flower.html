Care Instructions

  • Dust regularly: Use a soft brush or compressed air to gently remove dust from your forever flowers.
  • Avoid direct sunlight: While our flowers won't wilt, prolonged exposure to direct sunlight may cause colors to fade over time.
  • Keep away from moisture: Paper and fabric flowers should be kept in dry areas to maintain their shape and color.
  • Handle with care: Though durable, handle your forever flowers gently to preserve their delicate details.
  • Store properly: If you need to store your flowers, place them in a box with tissue paper to protect them.
